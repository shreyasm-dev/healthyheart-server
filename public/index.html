Installing As an App (instructions may not work on older versions of browsers or OSes)

First, go to the app, then:
  • iOS and iPadOS:
    • Safari - Tap the share () button. Scroll to the right in the bottom row of the share menu until you see "Add to Home Screen" and click it. Tap "Add" on the top-right of the screen.
    • (Other browsers not supported)
  • Android:
    • Chrome - Tap "Add to home screen", and follow the on-screen instructions
    • Edge - Tap the overflow menu button () and tap "Add to home screen"
  • Desktop:
    • Chrome and Edge - Click the plus button () or a "computer with a down arrow" button in the right of the address bar
    • Firefox - Not supported
    • Safari - Not supported
